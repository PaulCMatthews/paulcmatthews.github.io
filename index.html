Differential Equations, Bifurcations and Chaos

Paul C. Matthews

This is the author website for the book Differential Equations, Bifurcations and Chaos, published by Springer Nature in September 2025 as part of their Springer Undergraduate Mathematics Series.

The book is aimed at undergraduate students in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and Engineering. Because of the broad target audience, the book does not include formal mathematical proofs.
